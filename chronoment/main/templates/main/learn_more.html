How Chronoment Works

For Senders

Receive a unique invitation link from an organiser. Use this link to privately upload your photos, videos, and heartfelt messages. Your contributions will be stored securely until the reveal date.

For Receivers

On the special reveal date, scan the QR code provided by the organiser. This will unlock a beautiful, private slideshow of all the collected memories, a truly unique gift!

Why Chronoment?

  • Private & Secure: All contributions are kept private until the reveal date.
  • Easy to Use: Simple invitation process for organisers and intuitive upload for senders.
  • Memorable Gift: Create a truly unique and heartfelt memory collection for any occasion.
  • Versatile Media: Supports text messages, images, and videos (including YouTube links).
